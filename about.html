About Srinagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is a city that exudes an ethereal charm with its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Nestled in the picturesque Kashmir Valley, Srinagar is a place of unparalleled magnificence that leaves visitors spellbound.The city's crown jewel is undoubtedly the iconic Dal Lake. Stretching across vast expanses, the lake is adorned with vibrant houseboats known as "Shikaras" and floating gardens of lotus flowers. A ride on these traditional wooden boats offers a serene experience, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the breathtaking panorama of mountains and shimmering waters.
